You have fallen for a phishing attempt. Here are some tips to recognize phishing emails:

  1. Check the sender's email address for any irregular names.
  2. Hover over links without clicking to see the destination URL.
  3. Be wary of urgent or threatening words.
  4. Look for spelling and grammar mistakes in the email.
  5. Never enter sensitive information like username and passwords on a site you have been redirected.
